## Citation Usage Guidelines

**For Academic Papers:**
Cite as: Author(s), "Title," *Journal/Conference*, location, date, pages.

**For Standards:**
Cite as: Organization, "Standard Name," Document ID, location, date.

**For Software:**
Cite as: Developer, "Software Name," version, date, URL.

**Example Citation in Text:**
"As demonstrated by Kuchta et al. [1], static analysis of driver IOCTL handlers reveals widespread dangerous capabilities..."
